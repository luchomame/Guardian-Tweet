About the project

Our diverse team developed an application to benefit local communities in the event of a natural disaster or pandemic. Our application alerts users of local negative events which were detected by training machine learning algorithms to twitter activity near the user's location. It also allows users to network with their loved ones to show that they are safe and to check if they are safe as well. This was accomplished by combining our skillsets in front and back end web technologies along with machine learning and data analysis.
